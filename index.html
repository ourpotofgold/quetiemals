The Fight for Eyuforyia

The Story of Eyuforyia

The skies of Eyuforyia once shimmered with an eternal twilight. This peace was shattered by the Collectors, alien invaders siphoning the forest’s "hum" to power their mechanization.

Alongside Ice Heart and Hotly, a growing resistance of Zenith Guardians fights to reclaim their organic world.
