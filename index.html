Core System

Match-3 As Warfare

Every tile on the board is a fragment of Eyuforyia’s hum. Your matches don’t just trigger skills—they physically rewrite terrain, status, and even enemy maximum health. [file:1]

Combat Loop
Chain, Charge, Detonate

Match elemental tiles to charge Zenith ultimates, then unleash them to clear rows, convert nodes, and carve out safe “Holy Ground” on an increasingly hostile board. [file:1] Guardians like Earth Fox reshuffle dead boards into combo scenarios, while Void Architect locks bosses in place for devastating follow-ups. [file:1]

Design Notes

The goal is to keep every move expressive: even a “bad” match should progress charge, advance status, or manipulate the board towards your next power turn. [file:1]
