The Fight for Eyuforyia

The Story of Eyuforyia

The skies of Eyuforyia once shimmered with a captivating blend of violet and gold, creating an eternal twilight that made dew on the giant clover leaves sparkle like scattered diamonds. This ancient peace was shattered when the sky was violently torn open by three enormous geometric monolithic shaped vessels.

These are not travelers, but Collectors. They anchored massive black metal harpoons deep into the planet’s crust, seeping a glowing, neon-green liquid that transformed vibrant blue grass into metallic wires and soft earth into hexagonal plates. This spreading corruption, known as the Tech-Biome, aims to "optimize" life into data points and tools for an alien AI.

When young Glacy was taken to be processed, his brother Ice Heart, a wolf of the glaciers, refused to wait for a plan. Alongside Hotly the firebird and a growing resistance of Zenith Guardians, he leads a desperate fight to stop the siphons, restore the natural song of the woods, and reclaim their world from total mechanization.
