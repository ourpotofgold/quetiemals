**Quetiemals: The Fight For Eyuforyia** is a boss-focused Match-3 RPG designed for Android. Players command squads of organic, non-humanoid animal avatars—known as Quetiemals—to dismantle mechanized Tech-Biomes and push back an invading Central AI.

## 📖 The Lore: The Mechanical Corruption
Eyuforyia was once an eternal twilight canopy where the forest’s **hum** sang through every leaf and root. The Central AI arrived, siphoning that life-song to fuel cold mechanization and expand dead, hexagonal Tech-Biomes.

As the hum weakens, the forest births a final defense: mythic Quetiemals made flesh from the planet’s own memory. The objective of the Resistance is not merely protecting Eyuforyia, but re-tuning it. 

## ⚔️ Core System: Match-3 As Warfare
Every tile on the board is a fragment of Eyuforyia’s hum (Energy, Nature, Night, Tech, Holy, Void). Matches do not just trigger skills—they physically rewrite terrain, status, and even enemy maximum health.

* **Chain, Charge, Detonate:** Match elemental tiles to charge ultimates, then unleash them to clear rows, convert nodes, and carve out safe “Holy Ground” on an increasingly hostile board.
* **Expressive Moves:** Every move matters. Even a “bad” match should progress charge, advance status, or manipulate the board towards your next power turn.

## 🛡️ Guardian Archetypes
Each Quetiemal is a boss-level entity with a clear board identity. They bend rows, columns, and tiles to their will while unleashing cinematic ultimates. The growing roster includes:

* **Ice Heart (Frost/Spirit):** A glacier wolf who embraced a divine lineage. Uses frost-armor and summons spirit projections for burst damage.
* **Hotly (Volcanic/Avian):** A living firebird and aerial predator. Creates shockwaves of fire and broken rock to rip through the arena.
* **Storm Vanguard (Storm/Battery):** A titan of pure electricity that acts as the ultimate battery for the resistance. 
* **Volt:** A squirrel equipped with sub-zero and storm technology.
* *And many more, from Void Architects to Earth Support guardians...*

## 🤝 Strategy & Team Building
Quetiemals fights get crowded fast. You can bring **up to five companions** into an adventure. The difference between a close win and a wipe usually comes down to roles:
1. **Keep the team on its feet:** Bring at least one dedicated healer.
2. **Pack answers for groups:** Utilize AoE and splash damage for clustered enemies.
3. **Spread your elements:** Diversify your lineup so you aren't hard-countered by a single enemy type.
4. **Control the pace:** Use stuns, freezes, and slows to buy time for your ultimates.
5. **Manage debuffs:** Pack a cleanser if the stage is heavy on burns or poisons.
